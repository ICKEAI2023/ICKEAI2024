SCOPE

International Conference on Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence (ICKEAI-2024) aims to create a platform for the exploration, discussion, and dissemination of knowledge engineering in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). The conference will focus on various aspects of AI, including its impact on job creation on different industries, its potential for revolutionizing industries across sectors, and the role of knowledge sharing in fostering advancements in AI and Knowledge Engineering.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of International Conference on Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence (ICKEAI-2024) is to promote dialogue, collaboration, and innovation in the conference topics mentioned below. Additionally, ICKEAI-2024 aims to provide a platform for renowned scientists, professors, and experts in the field of Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence on all engineering domains to share their expertise, insights, and research findings. Keynote speakers will deliver talks on specialized topics related to Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence, thereby enriching the knowledge base of conference participants. Furthermore, ICKEAI-2024 strives to foster international collaboration by bringing together researchers, practitioners, and professionals from around the world. The conference serves as a networking platform where ideas are shared and partnerships are established to further advance the field of Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence on a global scale.

About Us

ST.PETER'S ENGINEERING COLLEGE

The St. Peter’s Engineering College, affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technical University, Hyderabad, Telangana, started its journey in 2007, is an AICTE approved “BMQR” ISO 9001 Certified Engineering Institute, and it took no time to emerge as a premier educational institution in the field of technological studies. This is attested by the outstanding students it has been able to nurture and produce over the years. The number of students on roll is approximately 4000. Well-equipped with excellent infrastructures with classrooms and laboratories with hi-end facilities, in addition to amenities for sports and other recreational facilities, the College offers undergraduate courses in technical subjects like Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning), Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The highly qualified teaching staff and extremely cooperative management staff contribute to the burgeoning repute of the College. The recognition of the excellence of the St. Peter’s Engineering College is attested by its Autonomous Status, which it has been enjoying since 2019, and NBA and NAAC ‘A’ accreditations. Besides, it is in possession of ARIIA recognition, 10th ranking in the category of Best Engineering College in Telangana from Education World, IIC accreditation.St.Peter’s Engineering College is a Premium Institutional Member of CII (CONFEDERATION OF INDIAN INDUSTRY) & NASSCOM. The Institution was ranked as the 11th Best Engineering College in Telangana State by Times Engineering Ranking Survey in 2022. The College has a unique track record of placements with leading multinational and corporate companies, with attractive salary packages and towards this end the students of the college are groomed especially for extraordinary communication skills, aptitude, reasoning and logical skills. Students are here given the liberty to explore their creativity, hone their logical and technical skills through brainstorming, experimental enterprises, etc., and through all this an all-round development of a student’s personality is what is ultimately aimed at and encouraged for. With a futuristic education in end, the College encourages research endeavors, and constantly strives for modernization and update of resources and infrastructures and exploration of diverse career opportunities.

Manuscript Submission and Publication Process

Full papers should describe novel research contributions with evaluation results and are limited to five (5) printed pages (10-point font) including figures, references and appendices (two additional pages are allowed with a per-page extra fee). Short papers, limited in length to four (4) pages, should be more visionary in nature and are meant to discuss new challenges and visions, showcase early research results, and explore novel research directions.

All submitted papers must be unpublished and not considered elsewhere for publication should be written in English. The Latex and MS-Word templates for paper submission are available in the following link:
Conference Paper Template Download Link

Prospective authors are invited to submit an original Full or Short paper in PDF format via the Microsoft CMT online submission system using the following link: Paper Submission Link

Original research papers, case studies, and abstracts pertaining to the conference tracks are all welcome submissions from authors. To ensure the caliber and applicability of accepted papers, every submission will go through a rigorous peer-review process.

Please note that, ICKEAI-24 reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g. removal from publication) if the paper is not presented at the conference.


All the accepted and presented papers are planned to publish in a Scopus indexed Book Series of Springer and other two scopus journals
